Culture

My culture is Chinese as I am of Chinese ethnicity. I am of New Zealand nationality, as I live and was born here, but my parents were both born in China. They migrated from China to New Zealand to pursue education and a better place to live. However, the rest of my family still resides in China.
Every year, my family still celebrates our culture during celebrations and festivals such as Chinese New Year, the Dragon Boat Festival, and the Mooncake Festival. We also eat traditional Chinese food such as dumplings, zongzi, and mooncakes to celebrate these traditions.
